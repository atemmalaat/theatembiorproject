About Me & The Mission

"Everyone—and every business—has a story. My goal is to help YOU share YOUR STORY!."

– Atem Bior

👋 Who I Am

I’m Atem Bior — a former D1 athlete & professional basketball player, Software developer, Content creator and storyteller at heart. Whether I’m coaching a grassroots basketball team, building a podcast network, or growing a YouTube channel, my mission stays the same:

To tell stories that change the world.

From the hardwood to the home studio, I believe that authentic stories—told with passion and purpose—can shift culture, open doors, and leave a legacy.

🔌 What I’m Building

🏀 Slim Reapers

A grassroots AAU basketball program empowering youth through mentorship, training, and exposure. It’s about more than wins—it's about building young men: through discipline, and belief.

🎙️ The Searchers Podcast

A platform for deep conversations on culture, creativity, identity, and purpose. Where raw storytelling meets unfiltered curiosity.

🎥 MadeByKings

A creative media channel celebrating hoopers, developers, and creators. Built to amplify voices, document journeys, and shift culture through content.

🧠 Why It Matters

All three of these projects come from the same place: a belief in the power of storytelling to transform lives. Whether it's helping a teenager see a future beyond his postcode, giving a guest the mic to speak their truth, or building something that inspires just one person to keep going—that’s the win.

This site is more than a portfolio. It’s a mission in motion.
